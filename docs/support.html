Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI analysis work?

Record yourself shadowboxing or hitting a bag, then upload the video. Our AI analyzes your stance, guard, punches, footwork, and more, providing detailed scores and personalized recommendations for improvement.

Is my video data secure?

Yes. Videos are encrypted during transmission, processed for analysis, and not stored permanently. We never use your videos for AI training. You can delete all your data anytime in Settings.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your device Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí Boxing Coach AI ‚Üí Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Can I get a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. For refund requests, visit reportaproblem.apple.com or contact Apple Support.

How many videos can I analyze per day?

Free users: 3 analyses per day. Basic subscribers: Unlimited. Pro subscribers: Unlimited with priority processing.

What's the difference between Basic and Pro?

Pro includes everything in Basic plus: priority processing (faster analysis), advanced technique insights, support for longer videos (up to 2 minutes), and detailed exportable progress reports.

The app says my video is too large. What do I do?

Keep videos under 100MB and 60 seconds (Basic) or 2 minutes (Pro). Try recording at 1080p instead of 4K, or trim the video to the most important parts.

Why was my analysis inaccurate?

For best results: ensure good lighting, record from a side angle, wear contrasting clothes, and avoid crowded backgrounds. If multiple people are in frame, you'll be asked to select yourself.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Delete My Data. This permanently removes your account and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Email us at support@cienrios.com with details about what happened, your device model, and iOS version. Screenshots or screen recordings help us fix issues faster.
